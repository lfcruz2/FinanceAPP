Personal Finance & Investment Advisor App

AI-Powered Investment Guidance for Modern Investors

Executive Summary

The Personal Finance & Investment Advisor App is a React Native cross-platform mobile application designed to democratize sophisticated financial guidance through AI-powered insights. The app addresses critical pain points faced by modern investors, particularly tech-savvy Millennials and Gen Z users, by providing personalized portfolio analysis, sector-based recommendations, and educational content in an intuitive, mobile-first experience.

MVP Core Features & Epic Breakdown

Epic 1: Secure Onboarding & Unified Dashboard

Priority: Must-Have

Seamless user registration, financial account aggregation, consolidated dashboard with net worth, cash flow, and investment overview.

Key Benefits: Centralized financial view, reduced complexity, foundational trust building

Epic 2: AI-Powered Investment Guidance

Priority: Must-Have

Interactive risk assessment, goal definition, AI-driven ETF portfolio recommendations with contextual education.

Key Benefits: Demystifies investing, personalized starting point, builds foundational knowledge

Epic 3: Portfolio Tracking & Basic Insights

Priority: Must-Have

Monitoring of linked accounts, asset allocation visualization, performance tracking, AI-driven insights like diversification score.

Key Benefits: Clear performance understanding, portfolio composition insights, improvement identification

Epic 4: Stock & ETF Discovery with Paper Trading

Priority: Should-Have

User-friendly exploration via curated lists, basic profiles, simplified charts, integrated educational snippets, paper trading.

Key Benefits: Risk-free learning, market dynamics understanding, confidence building

Risk Analysis & Strategic Recommendations

Key Financial Risks

High Initial Burn Rate: $176K monthly costs require substantial funding runway

Customer Acquisition: Need efficient channels to achieve 110K+ users for breakeven

Conversion Optimization: 10% total conversion rate (8% + 2%) is ambitious for fintech

Churn Management: Must maintain low churn to sustain growth trajectory

Strategic Recommendations

Phased Launch: Start with Core features, add Premium gradually

Cost Optimization: Use more affordable alternatives for non-critical services

Pricing Strategy: Consider annual discounts to improve LTV/CAC ratio

Market Validation: Extensive beta testing to validate product-market fit

Funding Requirements

Series A: $8-12M for 18-24 months runway

Series B: $20-30M to reach profitability and scale

Total Estimated: $30-40M to reach sustainable profitability

Timeline: 3-4 years to reach positive cash flow

Success Metrics

User Growth: 15-20% monthly growth in active users

Conversion Rate: Achieve 10%+ free-to-paid conversion

LTV/CAC Ratio: Target 3:1 ratio for sustainable growth

Net Revenue Retention: >110% through upsells and expansion

Investment Conclusion
